    Ride Worldwide is made for Cyclists All over the world. It allows users to input their location into a form on the Home Page & search for the nearest public city bikes that they can use. Upon viewing the result on the page, users can click on the "Get Directions" button at the bottom of the result for a new tab with google maps route directly to the locatio of the city bike you found!

    The creation of Rider Finder is to simply help cyclists around the globe at anytime!
